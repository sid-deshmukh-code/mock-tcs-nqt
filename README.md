## Features

- 309 questions across Quant, Logical Reasoning, Verbal, DSA, Programming, Networking, OS, Databases, Cloud, AI/ML, and more
- Instant one-liner explanations after each answer
- 90-minute countdown timer
- Topic filter — practice one section at a time
- Progress auto-saved to your browser — resume anytime, no account needed
- Full answer review with section-wise score breakdown
- Works completely offline after first load — no server, no tracking, no data collection

---

## Privacy

All progress is stored in your **browser's localStorage** — it never leaves your device. Each person who opens the link gets their own independent session saved locally on their own machine.
